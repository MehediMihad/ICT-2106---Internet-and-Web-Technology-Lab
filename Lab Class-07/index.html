Dhaka is the capital of Bangladesh. The Dhaka Division, located in the central part of the country, is the political, economic, and cultural heart of Bangladesh, hosting the capital city, Dhaka.

Chattogram Division, in the southeast, is home to the country’s largest seaport and includes the scenic hill tracts and the world-famous Cox’s Bazar sea beach

In the northwest Rajshahi Division, lies known for its educational institutions, silk industry, and mango orchards.

The Khulna Division, in the southwest, boasts the Sundarbans—the largest mangrove forest in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
